# Your facts

Everyday facts CoApply can use to fill in applications — where you're based, the
comp you're targeting, your work-authorization status, and the like.

**This is not a vault.** Like the rest of your profile, anything here is sent to the
AI when it writes for you. That's fine for these facts — the AI needs them to write
naturally ("based in Austin, authorized to work in the US"). So put facts that help
your applications, and keep real secrets out.

**Good to put here:** target salary / comp range, city, openness to relocation,
work-setup preference (remote / hybrid / onsite), work-authorization status,
sponsorship needs, earliest start date / notice period.

**Never put here:** SSN, passwords, bank / card / account numbers, API keys, or your
exact street address. CoApply refuses to store those.
